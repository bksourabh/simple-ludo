Simple Ludo

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Simple Ludo ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any external servers.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID user identifier and optionally your name (if you choose to share it). This information is stored locally on your device only.
  • Game Data: All game progress, saved games, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We do not collect device information or analytics.
  • Game Center: If you use online multiplayer, Apple Game Center handles matchmaking and player identity. Game Center data is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide game functionality (offline and online play)
  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Display your name in-game when signed in with Apple
  • Enable online multiplayer matchmaking via Game Center

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your data (game saves, preferences, authentication state) is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults framework.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload your data to any external servers.
  • Sign in with Apple: Authentication is handled securely by Apple's systems. We only store a user identifier locally.
  • Game Center: Online multiplayer communication is handled peer-to-peer through Apple's GameKit framework.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following Apple services:

  • Sign in with Apple: For secure, private authentication
  • Game Center: For online multiplayer matchmaking, achievements, and leaderboards

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data directly within the app from the main menu
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Sign out of your account at any time from the main menu
  • Manage your Sign in with Apple credentials in your Apple ID settings
  • Manage Game Center settings under Settings > Game Center on your device

Children's Privacy

Simple Ludo is a family-friendly board game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain any advertising, in-app purchases, or links to external content. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
